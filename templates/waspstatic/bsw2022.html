How you can be part of astronomy's big data revolution

As part of British Science Week, we're hosting a live panel discussion and Q&A where we will talk about the connection between the coming “big data” era in astronomy and the increasing importance of citizen science projects for training machine learning models.

Open University research student Adam McMaster will be joined by Stephen Serjeant, Hugh Dickinson, and Beatriz Mingo to discuss black holes, machine learning, and citizen science. We will encourage the audience to join us in working on one of our current projects, Black Hole Hunters.
